IPL may happen in Sept-Oct

The biggest T20 tournament of world, IPL was dated in Mar-May '20, but due to COVID-19 outbreak, the most loved followed tournament of Cricket has to take measures to ensure security of Players playing, staffs and officials. As BCCI Chairman, Saurav Ganguly said, IPL may be held during Sept-Oct.

Changes in Tournament

  • No overseas players, due to travel Restrictions.
  • Less Number of Matches.
  • No crowd and Audience, matches behind closed doors.
  • High TV rights to Hotstar and Stars Network.
